Outreach Programs

Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers

For 2020 I will have the privilege of being the academic adviser for the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers, an organization which seeks to promote young engineers from all fields.



Senior Design Projects

As part of their undergraduate education, Materials Science students at the University of Tennessee participate in a senior design project, working with an active research lab. Our lab has participated in this program by mentoring these teams.



Rural Youth for Science and Engineering (RYSE)

Our in-development K-12 and public outreach program to engage youth in science and technology programs. These communities tend to have poor exposure to exciting science demonstrations, which is key to drawing youth into STEM fields. This program will include a 6' demonstration Tesla coil, exotic materials (aerogels and vantablack), ferrofluids, as-well-as chemistry and aroma (food science) demonstrations.



Taste of Science Knoxville

Taste of Science is a nonprofit dedicated to promoting science among adults around the US. They have more locations joining in festivals every year with over 150 volunteers. It's a place for anyone with an interest in arts and sciences, and the culinary choices are just something extra.
